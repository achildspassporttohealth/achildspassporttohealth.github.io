Our Services

CP2H is a volunteer, not for profit organization, and welcomes referrals for donated medical and surgical services for children with surgical conditions not able to be treated in their home country. All referrals/requests for services will be initially screened by a CP2H representative and an initial response provided within 2 weeks to the referring person/agency.

Who We Serve

  • Age: 6 weeks to 17 years of age
  • Race/Religion: All races, ethnicity, religions
  • Income levels: Equivalent to: at or below federal poverty level as defined by US Federal Government
  • Children with surgical conditions able to be treated and cured, limited to no chronic, anticipated ongoing follow-up care
  • Children with Primary physician or provider willing to accept responsibility for coordination of care in host country. This includes child referrals in the USA
  • Children with primary contact person(s)/organization in host country willing to sponsor child and facilitate completing paperwork, documentation and travel arrangements. Contact person(s) and/or physician must have access to reliable and timely communication such as email or equivalent with requirement to respond to communication within 7 calendar days
  • Children only. We do not accept parents, guardians, hosts or sponsors for travel to the US

Who We Do Not Serve

  • Children/families with financial, emotional, and physical resources to receive care in their own country or within referral network of the host country (example Rwanda, South Africa)
  • Children who previously have received care for the same or related conditions by other specialists or referral networks
  • Children age 18 or greater at time of anticipated start of care date in the USA
  • Children with chronic ongoing illnesses that are anticipated to require ongoing, recurrent care such as multiple/serial surgical revisions or treatments, or that would require monitoring in the host country by medical personnel with expertise in the condition(s) being treated; conditions requiring long-term monitoring using diagnostic imaging/testing in host country.
  • Children with medical illnesses such as diabetes, pulmonary diseases, cancer and metastatic disease
For Children accepted to CP2H for care, every effort is made to provide the safest, highest quality of care and service. Our all-volunteer Board of Directors reviews the cases to determine if they meet criteria for our program and services. We do this based on the information and medical records we have available to us at the time of the referral. Once a child has been identified as meeting our criteria, we provide:
  • Donated Medical care
  • Emotional, medical and cultural support
  • The identification of resources
  • Academic, emotional & medical education.

For more information or to refer a child, please contact us.
